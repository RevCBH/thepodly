About Podly

Podly is a podcast indexer. We give podcasters simple tools (built on top of the index) that make their podcast easier for audiences to discover, engage, search, and share.

Podly was started in 2012 and put on pause to make a Bitcoin Exchange, Buttercoin. Now that Buttercoin has wound down I'm picking Podly back up. See 'Project History' section for more details.

Details

The interactive player above is assembled from an index of the HealYourselfRadio.com podcast.

The index contains the following info about relevant links:

  • Time mentioned (in seconds from the start)
  • Link name (as it should appear to an end user)
  • URL of the most relevant external web page
  • [not show here] Image most relevant to url / link name

*Podly's index can also be used for search and discovery, see below.

Search

We've indexed ~1,000 hours of audio / video content, primarily on 1 podcast, the Joe Rogan Experience (JRE). Each episode of the JRE is ~3 hours long and covers a wide range of topics. Finding a particular topic in these podcasts is nearly impossible without Podly. Podly makes search easy and nearly instant.

Social Share

Interacive podcast players (based on Podly's index) can be generated on the fly and embeded on twitter and Facebook, example below.

This is a BIG deal for podcasts.

One of the biggest problem podcasters face is increasing their reach. Podly's social sharing feature makes it easy for podcasts to be discovered by new viewers on the sites they already frequent like Facebook and Twitter.
